1. Introduction: A Contemporary Spark

2. The Problem of "Classical Homeopathy"

The phrase "classical homeopathy" is of relatively recent origin. It is often used to describe the Kentian tradition, with its emphasis on high potencies, lengthy case-taking, and reliance on metaphysical constructs.

3. Hahnemann's Scientific Homeopathy
